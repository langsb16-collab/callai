Basic Service Info
What is this service?
AI Call Assistant handles, records, and automatically summarizes and analyzes phone, voice, and messenger conversations.
When should I use it?
Perfect for sales calls, customer service, contract negotiations, international calls, and call record management.
Does AI actually answer calls?
Yes. AI can receive calls directly through virtual numbers and converse naturally like a human.
Recording & Summary
How are calls recorded?
Voice is converted to text in real-time and stored with the original audio.
What format is the summary?
A 3-5 line summary of key points with automatically organized agreements and action items.
AI Negotiation Assistant
What does the negotiation assistant do?
Analyzes counterparty statements during calls to provide negotiation strategies, suggested responses, and risk warnings.
Can it help with price negotiations?
Yes. It comprehensively analyzes price, schedule, and conditions to suggest optimal alternatives.
Security & Legal
Is call recording legal?
Automatic notification messages are applied according to recording laws by country.
Can it be used as legal evidence?
Timestamps and hash values are included, making them usable as evidence.
